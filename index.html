A  Little  About  Me

My name is Reda Hatine, a digital marketer, social media manager, and 3D designer passionate about innovation and digital transformation. I specialize in content marketing, data analytics, 3D design, and the integration of generative AI to create and execute high-impact digital strategies that enhance brand visibility, engagement, and performance. With international experience across Morocco, France, and Canada, I have successfully managed multichannel campaigns involving SEO, SEA, and email marketing, while producing creative and consistent digital content aligned with each brand’s identity. I’m a natural leader and persuasive communicator, committed to helping organizations grow through creativity, data-driven decisions, and authentic digital presence.
